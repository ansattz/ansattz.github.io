Autor

Hello, I'm Douglas, 23 years old, I'm from Rio de Janeiro and I'm currently graduating in mathematics at UFRJ. Also, I am a student in the Oracle Next Education (ONE) program, where I am improving my skills in Front end development.. My passion for mathematics came after philosophy and computing, where I was able to understand issues/discussions of ontology, phenomenology and epistemology, as well as notions of algorithms, information theory and others. Although I am currently more focused on the study of mathematics, I still take a lot of time to investigate both philosophy and computing topics. In particular, with respect to philosophy, my interests are in the history of philosophy and phenomenology. In computing, my interests are in data science and web development.
